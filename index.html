About

Decor is a full-service interior design company located in the Newtown area since 1990, specializing in both residential and commercial design. At Decor, we offer a wide range of furniture options to suit every style and preference. From stylish sofas and tables to elegant chairs and beds, our collection is carefully curated to ensure we provide only the finest pieces. In addition to our extensive furniture selection, we are proud to offer a range of excellent facilities to enhance your experience. Our team of experienced designers is available to assist you in transforming your space, offering personalized design solutions that meet your specific needs and budget.
